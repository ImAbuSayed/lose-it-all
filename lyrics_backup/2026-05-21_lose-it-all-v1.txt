[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Akhiyan ch nasha tera lagda ae sweet paradise,
Tu sadha lai meri ban jaaven without a price.
Main har pal takda haan tainu breaking the ice,
Mera dil kenda ae aaja rolling the dice.

[Hook 2]
Husan tera sohniye mainu karda ae fly,
Main tere utte hi marda haan no need to lie.
Sama vi ruk janda ae kudiye when you pass by,
Mera hosh ud janda ae touching the sky.

[Verse 1]
Raat de kachche hanere vich making my way,
Tere ishaareyan te chalda haan day after day.
Tere sohne bullan di muskaan takes breath away,
Main poora hi dull gaya haan what can I say.
Teri zulfan di ghani chhaan thalle hiding my pain,
Tainu har pal apni banuna ae going insane.
Main jag diyan gallan bhulawanga playing this game,
Mera ikk ikk saah chalda ae calling your name.

[Pre-Chorus]
Dooriyaan ae saadiyan mainu tearing me apart,
Main sacha ishq hi kamaya haan giving my heart.
Tere roop di nawi roshni ae like a fresh start,
Mera kamla dil dhadkaunda ae beautiful art.

[Chorus]
Main kalla tera aashiq haan the best I have seen,
Tere naina da gehra nasha vi lagda serene.
Mera seena vi tez dhadkunda ae like a machine,
Tu saari duniya ton wakhri ae beauty supreme.
Aaja tu hun meri baahan vich show me the way,
Main sachhi tenu hi chaunda haan day after day.
Mera sab kujh ud gaya ae what can I say,
Tere bina meri nawi sahan vi fading away.

[Verse 2]
Uchi engine di aawaz kudiye roaring so loud,
Main tez gaddiyan bhajaunda haan leaving the crowd.
Tere pichhe main tur aawanga feeling so proud,
Mera nawa rutba vi uncha ae floating on cloud.
Tu halke gusse vich khendi ae toxic romance,
Main pagal fir vi haan khadya taking a chance.
Mere pakke yaar naal rehnde ne watching my stance,
Tere sache ishq ch pagal haan in a deep trance.

[Verse 3]
Saare lokki kende ne mainu crazy for you,
Main poori duniya naal ladhanga loyal and true.
Mere akhan de hanju sukk gaye ne feeling so blue,
Main apna sab kujh gawa leya ae without a clue.
Kaale sheeshe de lukke pichhe haan hiding my tears,
Main jagg ton kalla hi ladya haan facing my fears.
Teri sachi ikk muskaan de lai wasting my years,
Mera kalla dil hun ronda ae nobody hears.

[Bridge]
Saeed da eh nawa geet kudiye sparking the fire,
Main tenu har pal takda haan my only desire.
Tu mere seene naal lagg jaaven taking me higher,
Mera sacha pyar roohani ae burning the wire.
Tu pakki meri hi ban jaaven feeling so right,
Main tenu uchi pukaarda haan deep in the night.

[Final Chorus]
Main kalla tera aashiq haan the best I have seen,
Tere naina da gehra nasha vi lagda serene.
Mera seena vi tez dhadkunda ae like a machine,
Tu saari duniya ton wakhri ae beauty supreme.
Sadi roohan di nawi taar judi holding so tight,
Main poora tera hi rahvanga into the light.
Tu pyar naal hath mera fadd le making it bright,
Mera sachha aakhri saah vi ae fighting the fight.
